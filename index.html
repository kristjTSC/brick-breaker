The Switching Bricks

Welcome to The Switching Bricks.
Here you will take controll of 2 paddles,
with your mission to destory bricks quickly.

One is controlled with A-D and one with Left-Right buttons.


But be careful! Every time you hear a "beep" cues, it means that
the input for paddles will switch!


So, don't forget to test the sound!


When you are ready click START button


Have fun and enjoy!
